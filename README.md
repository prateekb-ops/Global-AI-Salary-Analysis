# 📊 Global Tech & AI Salary Analysis (2025)

## 📝 Project Overview
An exploratory data analysis of the global Data Science and Artificial Intelligence job market. This project analyzes compensation trends, remote work distribution, and high-demand locations across various experience levels to uncover actionable insights about the industry.

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
* **Database:** SQLite
* **Language:** SQL (Aggregations, Filtering, Grouping, CASE Statements)
* **Data Source:** Kaggle (Data Science, AI & ML Job Salaries in 2025)

## 💡 Key Insights
* **Highest Paying Roles:** Leadership and specialized lead roles command the highest compensation. The top-paying position globally is Research Team Lead at $450,000, while specialized management roles like Applied AI ML Lead consistently average near $292,500.
* **Experience Impact:** Executive (EX) roles boast the highest average compensation at roughly $200,471. However, Senior (SE) roles hold the vast majority of the job market share with over 87,000 postings, averaging a highly competitive $172,658.
* **Work Models:** While Fully Remote jobs maintain strong salaries (averaging $151,286), On-Site roles offer both the highest volume of opportunities (119,570 postings) and the highest average pay ($159,383).
* **Global Hubs:** The United States overwhelmingly dominates the global data job market in both volume (135,569 postings) and average compensation ($163,905).
